Frequently Asked Questions

Is dog poop a fertilizer?

Most definitely NOT! Dog waste carries bacteria and parasites that can be harmful to humans and other animals.

How long can it take for dog waste to break down?

Dog waste can take over a year to degrade completely. The poop may no longer be visible, but eggs from parasites can linger for years in the soil.
